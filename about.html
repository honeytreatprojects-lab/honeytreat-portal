Our Story

Rooted in Experience, Driven by Passion

Honeytreat Trade Academy (HTA) began as a training outfit dedicated to equipping youth, construction workers, and artisans with practical skills for success in construction trades. Over the years, we evolved into a hub for skill advancement across multiple fields.

Today, HTA is expanding its expertise beyond construction to include Digital Skills, Entertainment, and, as of this year, Agriculture — empowering individuals to thrive in every sector of Nigeria's economy.

From the CEO's Desk

A Message of Welcome

Samson Soyebi CEO, Honeytreat Group

Greetings,

On behalf of Honeytreat Trade Academy, I warmly welcome you to an exciting journey into the future of skills, competence, and innovation in Africa and beyond.

"Whether your passion lies in traditional construction trades or the cutting-edge arts, Honeytreat Trade Academy's plan is nurturing an environment where growth, learning, and transformation takes center stage."

For over five years, we have proudly trained Nigerians in essential construction skills, empowering more than 2,500 youth to secure gainful employment. In response to the growing opportunities in the digital economy, we have expanded our curriculum to include digital skills, photography, music, and cinematography.

With the guidance of experienced instructors, state-of-the-art facilities, and strong industry partnerships, we are committed to helping you achieve your goals. Join us as we embark on a journey of success and inspiration together — let's build a brighter future, one skill at a time.

Samson Soyebi Chief Executive Officer, Honeytreat Group
Our Values & Purpose

Building a Better Future Through Values

HTA Mission

Our Mission

To empower individuals and transform society by making skills the catalyst for national productivity and prosperity — by bridging the gap between education and employment, creating industries and empowering individuals.

HTA Vision

Our Vision

A society where skill becomes a catalyst for national productivity and prosperity — where every Nigerian, regardless of background, has access to world-class training that opens doors to opportunity.

HTA Goal

Our Goal

To establish and sustain a world-class training institution that develops a skilled construction workforce for Africa and beyond — one that is globally recognized and locally impactful.

HTA Impact

Our Impact

Shaping lives through innovation, collaboration, and excellence — nurturing a skilled workforce for sustainable growth and shared prosperity across Nigeria and the African continent.
