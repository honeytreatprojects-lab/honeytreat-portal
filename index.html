Who We Are

Redefining Vocational Education for a Brighter Tomorrow

Honeytreat Trade Academy (HTA) is a training institution dedicated to advancing skills in Agriculture, Construction, Digital Skills, and Entertainment.

HTA's mission is to empower Nigerians through internationally recognized training programs.
